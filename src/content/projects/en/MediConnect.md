MediConnect is a comprehensive online medical appointment platform that connects patients with doctors through an interactive booking system. Patients can search doctors by specialty, check availability, and reserve appointments, while doctors manage their profiles and schedules. Featuring JWT authentication, automated notifications, and an admin dashboard for operational oversight, MediConnect delivers a production-ready healthcare solution.
